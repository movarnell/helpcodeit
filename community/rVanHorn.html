Rachel's Bootcamp Survival Guide

I've been there and I know how overwhelming it is! Here are the things I did and the tutorials, websites and resources I used that were the most helpful in my journey. I hope they are valuable for you too!

You can find me on LinkedIn, GitHub or my portfolio!

General Recommendations

Survival Tips & Tricks
  • If you're attending a Promineo Tech bootcamp, schedule a mentor session every week. EVEN if you don't think you need it. Mentors are great for helping with specific problems, yes, but they can also review things and give you challenges to keep sharpening your skills.
  • Make a study group!!!!! I should have put this one first, because it was such a massive help for me. Find some folks in your cohort who you can Zoom with regularly. You will need one another to help you debug projects and bounce ideas off of. My study group still chats regularly and we have created an amazing support system.
  • Ask questions in classes - if you are in a live class and have a question, ask it! Your instructor wants to help, they like having students interact with them. There are no stupid questions, just unanswered ones.
  • It's ok to be lost or confused, especially if you are completely new to all of this - I was! Keep doing the work, keep asking questions, you will get there.
  • Utilize office hours! Even if you don't think you have a question, go and listen! I learned so much by just showing up and listening/watching in office hours.
